Last updated: 1 Jan 2026

We do not knowingly collect more than we need to respond to you and run our business.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

We do not sell your personal information.

We don't share your personal information except in these limited cases:

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purposes described here, or as required by law. You can ask us to delete your information at any time using the contact details below.

Your choices and rights
